假如要打开文件夹 C:\Users\Bear\document\py_script

Windows+R 打开 run

输入 `cmd`

在 `>` 后边依次输入这些
``C:\Users\Bear>C:
C:\Users\Bear>cd\
C:\>cd users
C:\Users>cd Bear
C:\Users\Bear>cd document
C:\Users\Bear\document>cd py_script
C:\Users\Bear\document\py_script>jupyter notebook``
